## Overview
This project documents a phishing email investigation using the Greenholt case study.
The goal is to determine whether the email is malicious by reviewing its content, headers, and embedded links, then documenting a clear verdict like a real SOC investigation.

---

## Investigation Summary
- **Case Type:** Phishing email
- **Trigger:** Suspicious email requiring validation
- **Scope:** Single email analysis

The investigation focused on verifying the sender, identifying malicious indicators, and assessing user impact.

---

## Investigation Approach
The email was analyzed using a standard SOC workflow:

1. Review the email content for social engineering
2. Analyze email headers to validate the sender
3. Extract and review indicators such as domains and URLs
4. Enrich indicators using threat intelligence
5. Reach and document a final verdict
